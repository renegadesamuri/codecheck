## üö® IMPORTANT: iOS Icon Requirements

Your icon MUST:
- ‚úÖ Be 1024√ó1024 pixels minimum
- ‚úÖ Have NO transparency (solid background)
- ‚úÖ Have rounded corners (iOS adds these automatically, don't add them yourself)
- ‚úÖ Work in both light and dark mode
- ‚úÖ Be recognizable at 40√ó40 pixels
- ‚úÖ Follow Apple's Human Interface Guidelines

Your icon SHOULD NOT:
- ‚ùå Have text that's hard to read when small
- ‚ùå Have fine details that disappear when scaled
- ‚ùå Have pre-applied rounded corners (iOS does this)
- ‚ùå Use copyrighted symbols or trademarks
- ‚ùå Have transparency in the background

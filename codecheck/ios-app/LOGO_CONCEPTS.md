## Design Resources

### Free Logo Makers (Quick Solution):
1. **Canva** (canva.com) - Free tier with templates
2. **Looka** (looka.com) - AI-powered logo generation
3. **Hatchful by Shopify** - Free, no signup required
4. **IconKitchen** - Specifically for app icons

### Professional Designers:
1. **Fiverr** - $20-100 for app icon design
2. **99designs** - Logo contests starting at $299
3. **Upwork** - Hire freelance designers
4. **Dribbble** - Find and hire top designers

### iOS Icon Requirements:
- 1024×1024 px (App Store)
- 180×180 px (iPhone 3x)
- 120×120 px (iPhone 2x)
- 167×167 px (iPad Pro)
- 152×152 px (iPad 2x)
- 76×76 px (iPad 1x)

### Generation Tips:
1. Start with 1024×1024 canvas
2. Use transparent background or gradient
3. Ensure icon works at small sizes (40×40)
4. Test in both light and dark mode
5. Avoid fine details (they disappear when scaled)
6. Use bold, clear shapes
7. Limit to 2-3 colors max
